Puddle Jumpers Preschool

2.5 - 4 years old

Register now

Puddle Jumper children participate in a wide variety of activities to stimulate their minds and use all of their senses.


The Puddle Jumper program is multi philosophy and we focus on ensuring the children are interested and excited about what they are learning and participating in. We want to help instill a lifelong love of learning and exploring. Our qualified, kind, nurturing and experienced teachers ensure your children are safe and happy.

The teachers guide and encourage the children’s involvement in art, music, movement, baking, stories, outside play and many other fun activities. We use methods and materials from the Montessori and Reggio Emilia philosophies with a play based approach. When children are 4 years old they transition in to our Get Ready for School afternoon preschool program.
