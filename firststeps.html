First Steps Playgroup

18 months - 2.5 years old

Register now

First Steps Playgroup is our youngest minds coming together to play, socialize, learn and have lots of fun. Our kind and nurturing teachers create and implement fun and interesting activities.


This program can be parent/caregiver participation if the parents/caregivers would like to stay until such time as everyone feels the child is ready to be left on their own. It is a wonderful opportunity for parents and caregivers to connect with others in the community with children of a similar age.

The goal is to transition children into our Puddle Jumpers program when they are 2 ½ years old.

The children participate in many fun activities including:

  • art and music
  • circle time
  • baking
  • bubble play
  • sand play
  • water play and much more

Please contact us if you'd like to learn more about this program.
