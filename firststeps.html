First Steps

18 months - 2.5 years old

Register now

Our kind and creative teachers create and implement fun and interesting activities each day such as; music, dance, stories, water and sand play, baking, painting, and crafts.

Parents/gaurdians and teachers work together to determine when children are ready to be left on their own in First Steps. Parents/guardians are always welcome to stay and participate. The goal is to transition children into our Puddle Jumper program when they are 2 ½ years old.
