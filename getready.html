Get Ready for School Preschool

4 - 5 years old

Register now

Our Get Ready for School program enables 4 and 5 year olds to continue with a play based class where they are able to use their imagination, independence and creativity while being involved in more structured activities to prepare for the exciting transition to kindergarten.


There is amore time spent on numbers, letters, writing, science experiments etc. as the children are at an age where they are better able to focus and work as a team. Daily, the children take part in a very interactive circle time with music, name games, calendar, songs and stories. We spend lots of time outside and there always many activities inside and out, to choose from.
