Out of School Care

For school age kids from Kindergarten to Grade 7.

After school the children are picked up on foot from Mckechnie and by bus from Maple Grove. A nutritious snack is provided when the children arrive and there is time for some homework or quiet reading and play.

Each day we have specific preplanned activities based on the childrenâ€™s ages and interests as well as a variety of games, toys, and art supplies which are always on hand. Some of the activities we have been doing lately include; drama, music (with a DJ!), baking, pizza making, soccer, hockey, obstacle courses, ping pong tournaments and the list goes on.

When the sun is shining, regardless of our plans, we often end up outside with the children participating in a spur of the moment hockey or soccer game, or playing in the school playground. We also provide care on school closure days, summer holidays and spring break for an additional fee.
